About Us

Transforming Systems was established in 2009 as a result of the delivery challenges observed in preparation for, and in response to, the H1N1 Swine flu pandemic. In a Joint venture with the University of Greenwich School of Computing & Mathematical Sciences Transforming Systems took the emerging lessons and developed shrewd - the single health resilience early warning database. Since 2009, we have grown into a smart and flexible company that can expand or contract its capacity through its developer and consultant network. We have also set up an overnight development facility.

Our Approach

We take an agile and human systems approach to any work we do which means that people are the most important part of any system, and our response to challenges is not predetermined. Indeed, many of the recommendation for systems improvement that we design do ’not‘ require an IT solution.

Who Are We

The senior management has experience in all sectors at senior levels including the Cabinet Office, Government departments, the European Commission, private sector IT companies, the charitable sector and academia.

Shrewd Resilience

Why was Shrewd developed?

  • Shrewd was developed to address the lessons learned from the response to the H1N1 pandemic and seasonal pressure reviews.
  • Lack of agreed indicators that are shared across a health economy.
  • Siloed planning and response to system resilience and critical data not being shared.
  • Poor communication systems across organisations, information rarely delivered in a timely fashion. Reporting process was flawed.
  • Slow speed of operational response due to poor data.
  • Lack of an audit trail.
  • No facility for senior managers to have line of sight system visibility.
  • Limited strategic system oversight for those needing to make an operational or strategic response.
  • Inability to benchmark system stress data across comparable organisations.
  • Too many single points of failure and ‘building in’ resilience seen as too costly
